About

GIVE is a dynamic and user-friendly volunteering platform dedicated to connecting passionate individuals with meaningful opportunities to make a positive impact in their communities and beyond. Founded on the belief that everyone has something valuable to contribute, GIVE simplifies the process of discovering, joining, and organizing volunteer activities across a wide range of causes — from environmental conservation and education to healthcare, social justice, and disaster relief. Whether you are a student looking to gain experience, a professional seeking to give back, or a retiree wanting to stay engaged, GIVE empowers you to find opportunities that align with your skills, interests, and availability. Our platform features an intuitive search and matching system, seamless event registration, impact tracking, and a vibrant community forum that fosters collaboration and lasting connections. At GIVE, we believe that collective action starts with individual commitment. By leveraging modern technology, we aim to lower barriers to volunteering, increase participation rates, and amplify the positive change volunteers create every day. Join the movement — GIVE your time, talent, and energy to a cause that matters.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make volunteering accessible, impactful, and rewarding for everyone. We strive to bridge the gap between people who want to make a difference and the organizations and communities that need their help. Through innovative technology and a people-first approach, GIVE removes traditional barriers to volunteering — such as lack of awareness, complicated sign-ups, and fragmented opportunities — so that more individuals can contribute their unique skills and time to causes they care about.

Our Vision

We envision a world where volunteering is a natural part of everyday life. A world in which every person can easily find and participate in meaningful service, organizations can efficiently mobilize support for their causes, and communities thrive through the power of collective action. GIVE aims to become the leading global platform that inspires millions to give back, fostering stronger, more connected, and more compassionate societies.

Our Values

  • Accessibility: We believe volunteering should be easy for everyone, regardless of background or experience.
  • Impact: Every action counts — we focus on measurable outcomes and real community benefit.
  • Community: We build connections that go beyond single events, creating lasting relationships and support networks.
  • Inclusivity: We welcome volunteers from all walks of life and celebrate diverse skills and perspectives.
  • Innovation: We harness technology to simplify processes and maximize the positive change volunteers can create.
